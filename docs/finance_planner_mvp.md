# Personal & Business Finance Planner

## Product Concept

A simple financial planning and tracking tool designed for individuals and small businesses in African markets. 

**Core Idea:**  
> Understand your money today, plan your money tomorrow.

---

## Problem

Most budgeting apps are built for Western systems and do not fit:
- Mobile money usage
- Cash-heavy economies
- Irregular income patterns

---

## Target Users

### Individuals
- Young professionals
- Freelancers
- Students

### Businesses
- Small business owners
- Freelancers
- Side hustlers

---

## Core Value Proposition

- Track money easily
- Plan future finances
- Make better financial decisions

---

## MVP Features

### 1. Authentication
- Sign up / login
- Session management

### 2. Accounts
- Personal account
- Business account
- Balance tracking

### 3. Transactions
- Add income
- Add expenses
- Categories
- Notes & dates
- Edit/delete

### 4. Categories
- Default categories
- Optional custom categories

### 5. Budgeting (Key Feature)
- Set monthly budgets
- Planned vs actual comparison
- Remaining budget tracking

### 6. Savings Goals
- Create goals
- Track progress

### 7. Reports
- Income vs expenses
- Spending by category
- Monthly trends

### 8. Dashboard
- Total balance
- Recent transactions
- Budget progress

---

## Differentiator

**Forward Planning**
- Expected income
- Expected expenses
- Forecasting vs actual results
